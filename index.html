Chapel Hill’s Home for Fresh, Hand‑Crafted Bakes

Nestled steps from Franklin Street, our family bakery has been sweetening Chapel Hill since 1997. From sunrise sourdoughs to late‑night macarons, every treat is mixed, rolled, and baked on‑site with local North Carolina ingredients and a pinch of Tar Heel pride.

Collaboration Policy

  1. Partners within 100 mi of Chapel Hill.
  2. Choose at least one local charity to benefit (e.g., NCDA, ServSafe).
  3. Shared focus on sustainability, inclusion, and food safety.
  4. Quarterly Q & A flyers with our head pastry scientists.
  5. Co‑marketing: IG takeovers, vendor events, fundraisers.
  6. Mutual benefit: adds value to both parties and our guests.
